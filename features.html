Explore UTSAVIA's Powerful Features

Every tool you need to organize, sponsor, and experience unforgettable college fests—explained in depth.

Multi-Role Access

UTSAVIA provides dedicated, secure portals for Colleges, Students, and Sponsors. Each user type gets a tailored dashboard, personalized tools, and a unique experience—ensuring everyone has what they need to succeed.

  • Separate logins and dashboards for each role
  • Role-based permissions and access control
  • Personalized notifications and workflows

Smart Dashboards

Get real-time analytics, actionable insights, and management tools customized for your needs. Track event progress, sponsorship status, and team performance at a glance.

  • Live event and sponsorship tracking
  • Customizable widgets and reports
  • Visual analytics for quick decision-making

AI Matchmaking

Our intelligent algorithms connect the right sponsors with the right college events, based on audience fit, brand goals, and event themes. Maximize sponsorship value and event success.

  • Automated sponsor-event recommendations
  • Audience and brand profile matching
  • Continuous learning for better results

Advanced Reporting

Access comprehensive post-event analytics, downloadable performance reports, and ROI calculations. Showcase your event’s impact to stakeholders and sponsors.

  • Attendance, engagement, and financial metrics
  • Exportable PDF and Excel reports
  • ROI dashboards for sponsors and colleges

Seamless Collaboration

Integrated chat, task management, and automated workflows keep your team, sponsors, and vendors aligned. Communicate, assign tasks, and track progress—all in one place.

  • Real-time messaging and notifications
  • Task assignment and progress tracking
  • Automated reminders and workflow triggers

Vendor Marketplace

Browse and book from a curated directory of event vendors—catering, production, artists, and more. Manage contracts, payments, and reviews directly through the platform.

  • Verified vendor profiles and ratings
  • Integrated booking and payment system
  • Vendor management dashboard

Fest Enablement for All

UTSAVIA helps colleges that have never organized a fest to plan, fund, and execute successful events. Our onboarding, templates, and expert support ensure every student gets the fest experience they deserve.

  • Step-by-step onboarding for new organizers
  • Customizable event templates and guides
  • Dedicated support from the UTSAVIA team
